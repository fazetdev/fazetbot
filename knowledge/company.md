# Company Information

## Legal Status
Fazet Edutech Tech Ltd is a legally registered technology company in Nigeria.

## Company Overview
Fazet Edutech Tech Ltd is a Nigerian technology company specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI), business automation, web application development, website development, and custom software solutions.

The company develops intelligent digital products and provides technology services that help businesses, educational institutions, startups, and organizations improve efficiency, automate repetitive processes, enhance customer engagement, and accelerate digital transformation.

In addition to custom software development, Fazet Edutech Tech Ltd designs and maintains proprietary software products that address real-world operational challenges across multiple industries.
