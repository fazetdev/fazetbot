# Product Overview

The AI Chatbot Platform is an intelligent conversational system developed by Fazet Edutech Tech Ltd to help businesses, schools, organizations, and institutions automate customer interactions using Artificial Intelligence.

The platform enables users to ask questions in natural language and receive fast, relevant, and context-aware responses based on the organization's knowledge and business information.

---

# Purpose

The platform was developed to improve customer communication by providing instant, automated, and accurate responses while reducing the workload of customer support teams.

It helps organizations make important information available 24 hours a day without requiring constant human intervention.
