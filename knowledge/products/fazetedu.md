# Product Overview

FazetEdu.ng is a digital platform designed for Nigerian schools. It combines a professional school website with essential administrative tools that help schools improve communication, simplify admissions, manage fees, publish academic results, and strengthen their online presence.

The platform enables schools to modernize their operations without the complexity of traditional school management systems.

---

# Purpose

FazetEdu.ng was developed to help schools establish a professional digital presence while providing practical tools for everyday administrative tasks.

The platform aims to:

- Improve communication between schools and parents.
- Simplify student admissions.
- Support fee management.
- Publish academic information online.
- Enhance the school's professional image.
- Reduce manual administrative work.
