# Product Overview

The AI WhatsApp Automation Platform is an intelligent business communication solution that combines Artificial Intelligence with WhatsApp to automate customer interactions, business workflows, and routine communication.

The platform enables businesses to provide instant customer support, answer frequently asked questions, collect information, qualify leads, schedule appointments, and automate repetitive business processes through WhatsApp.

---

# Purpose

The platform was developed to help organizations automate customer communication while maintaining fast, professional, and consistent interactions on WhatsApp.

It reduces manual work, improves response times, and enables businesses to serve customers more efficiently.
