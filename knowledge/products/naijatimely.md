# Product Overview

NaijaTimely is an online booking and appointment management platform developed for Nigerian businesses. It helps service-based businesses accept online bookings, organize appointments, reduce scheduling conflicts, and improve customer experience through a simple booking system.

The platform allows businesses to create a personalized booking page that customers can access online without needing to contact the business manually.

---

# Purpose

NaijaTimely was created to simplify appointment management for Nigerian businesses by replacing manual booking processes with an organized digital solution.

The platform helps businesses:

- Accept bookings online.
- Organize daily schedules.
- Reduce double bookings.
- Reduce missed appointments.
- Improve customer communication.
- Save administrative time.

# How NaijaTimely Works

## Step 1

The business creates an account.

---

## Step 2

The business adds:

- Services
- Staff
- Working hours
- Availability

---

## Step 3

NaijaTimely generates a unique booking page.

---

## Step 4

- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- Facebook
- Website
- Other social media

---

## Step 5

Customers select:

- Service
- Date
- Available time

---

## Step 6

---

## Step 7

Notifications and reminders are sent where applicable.
