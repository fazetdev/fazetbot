# Company Policies

## Project Engagement

Every project begins with a consultation to understand the client's objectives, requirements, and expected outcomes before development begins.

---

## Project Scope

A clearly defined project scope is agreed upon before implementation. Requests for additional features or major changes after approval may require a revised quotation or timeline.

---

## Communication

Regular project updates are provided throughout development. Clients are encouraged to provide timely feedback to support smooth project delivery.

---

## Quality Assurance

Every solution undergoes testing before deployment to ensure functionality, reliability, and usability.

---

## Confidentiality

Client information, project details, and business data are treated as confidential and are not shared with unauthorized third parties.

---

## Intellectual Property

Ownership of project deliverables is determined by the agreement made between Fazet Edutech Tech Ltd and the client before or during the project.

---

## Maintenance and Support

Support services may include bug fixes, technical assistance, system updates, and agreed maintenance activities after project delivery.

---

## Ethical AI

We develop AI solutions responsibly and encourage the ethical use of Artificial Intelligence in ways that support businesses, organizations, and society.
