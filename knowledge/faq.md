### What is NaijaTimely?

NaijaTimely is an online booking and appointment management platform designed for Nigerian businesses. It enables businesses to accept online bookings, manage appointments, send WhatsApp notifications, and organize customer schedules.

---

### What is FazetEdu.ng?

FazetEdu.ng is a digital platform for Nigerian schools that combines a professional school website with tools for admissions, fee tracking, result publishing, and school announcements.
